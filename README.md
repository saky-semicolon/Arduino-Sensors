# Arduino-Sensors
It's a compact yet comprehensive repository that delves into the world of sensors compatible with Arduino microcontrollers. It's a great resource for beginners and experts alike. It's a work in progress and will be updated regularly.

<img src="Cover Pic.jpg" width="100%">

Arduino is a popular open-source electronics platform that allows you to create a wide variety of projects, including those involving sensors. Sensors are essential components in many Arduino projects as they enable the Arduino to interact with its environment by measuring physical quantities such as temperature, light, motion, and more. Here are some common types of sensors used with Arduino.

PS: These are just a few examples of the many sensors available for Arduino projects. Depending on your project's requirements, you can choose the appropriate sensor to collect data and interact with the physical world. Arduino provides a variety of libraries and resources to help you interface with these sensors and make your projects come to life.
